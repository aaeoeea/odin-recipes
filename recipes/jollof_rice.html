Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is more than a dish; it's easily the most popular Nigerian food

Cultural wars and battles are perennially fought on social media to determine which West Africa country makes the best and the most delicous Jollof Rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Bell pepper
  • 3-5 Tomatoes
  • Buillion cube for seasoning
  • 1 Large Onion
  • Cray fish

How to make Jollof Rice

  1. Please follow steps itemized in the New YorK Times
  2. Best served with a chilled glass of Heineken plus Lojay's Monalisa with Ampiano huming in the background
