Yam and Egg

A Popular Carribean and West African Staple

Of several dishes made from yam including Pounded Yam, Porridge and Fries, Yam served with scrambled eggs is the most popular for breakfast in several homes in West Africa and beyond. It's a balanced diet which is easy and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • 3-5 eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Bell pepper
  • 3-5 Tomatoes
  • Buillion cube for seasoning
  • 1 Large Onion

How to make Yam & Egg

  1. Peel and slice the yam into circular discs
  2. After washing, place the sliced yams into a large pot and add enough water to cover them
  3. Add sliced onions(1/4 of 1 large onion) and 1/2 teaspoon of salt, then boil for 20-25 minutes
  4. While the yam is boiling, slice the Tomatoes, Bell Pepper and the remaining Onions. Add them to a moderately heated Vegetable oil (3-4 teaspoons) and cook for 2-3 mins over medium heat
  5. Add the wisked eggs plus buillion cube and a pint of salt
  6. cook and stir occasionally until liquid egg disappears (~ 5min)
  7. Once the Yam is boiled, drain the water and served with fried egg
