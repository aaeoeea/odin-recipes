Moin Moin

A traditional Nigerian main dish made from black-eyed peas

Moin Moin is a protein-rich Nigerian staple made from steamed beans seasoned with cray fish, eggs, fish and assorted meat.

Ingredients

  • Black eyed peas/Brown beans
  • Habanero
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Bell pepper
  • 3-5 Tomatoes
  • Buillion cube for seasoning
  • 1 Large Onion

How to make Moin Moin

  1. Please follow steps itemized by Chef Immaculate
  2. Best served with Bread or Gaari
